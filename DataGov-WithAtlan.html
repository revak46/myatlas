DBA Lead — Day 0 Assessment

The SQL estate contains well-defined structure but almost no self-describing business metadata. Sensitive data candidates exist across 3,420 columns — only 8% are formally tagged. 241 databases have no steward. Cross-system lineage is invisible from SQL alone. Manual governance at this scale is not operationally viable. The risk surface is real, measurable, and growing at 8.7% per quarter.

DBA Lead — Day 90 Assessment

After 90 days of Atlan catalog ingestion and steward enrichment, the governance posture has shifted from reactive to managed. Sensitive data classification moved from 8% to 71%. Cross-system lineage — previously invisible — now covers 89% of flagged assets. Stewardship is assigned across 86% of databases. The governance readiness score improved from 18 to 67 out of 100. The remaining gaps represent the next phase backlog, not an unmanaged unknown. The SQL estate is now discoverable, classifiable, and audit-ready at scale.
